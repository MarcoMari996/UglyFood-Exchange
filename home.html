The Project

we are an e-commerce platform by which markets can sell any kind of food otherwise it would have been wasted because, for example, it's still unsold at the end of the working-day. Think about 'ugly foods': all around the world hundreds of tons of food are wasted just because of their bad appearance.

Here we try to provide a new way of recycling unsold foods, by selling/buying it at a discounted price through this platform.
